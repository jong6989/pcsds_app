Uploading Files

On the middle right corner is the list of created draft documents by default. You can click on the item to open it on the right pane.


You can edit the content anytime as long as it is on draft. The data are auto saved.


This grey box is a dropable area for files or you can click the button to select files. Uploading files are done seamlessly when you are online. Just Select your file, and thats its!


An indicator will tell you if your files are successfully uploaded to the cloud server.


You can open and update your file as long as it is not yet published. But make sure to update it by clicking the yellow button.
