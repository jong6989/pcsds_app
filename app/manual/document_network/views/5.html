Publishing / Sending Document
Requirements for publishing a document
  1. User Account must be activated by the Agency HR/Admin.
  2. Draft document should contain file/files.
  3. Files must be uploaded online before publishing. (with green check)

Select your agency if connected with multiple agencies.

Confirm your action and your document will turn into published mode.


Document cant be edited at this rate, but you can return it to DRAFT mode for editing.


Click the send button for selecting receipient and sending.


For multiple access, you can select "Send as" dropdown item for available receipients.

Note: receipients may differ according to access level.

Select receipient and write your remarks.
For individual remarks, you just select one receipient and click the send button.



A toast message will apear notifiying your sent docs.



Sent items are listed and can be monitored for status.
