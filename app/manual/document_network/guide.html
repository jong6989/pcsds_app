Document Network

Starting Up

On the upper left corner, click the three line button to open the menu pane.


Select abd Click "Document Network" button to enter.


Wait until it loads..


For 1st time users, a registration form is required to be filled up.


Now You are inside the Document Network System. You can start creating a draft document.

Creating Draft

On the upper right corner, click the "add new draft" button to open a form.


Fill out the form.



Source : can be your name, or where the document originated.

Date A : can be the corespondence date or date the document is created.

Date B : can be the Receiving date or date the document is completed.

Category : lets you categorize your documents.

Uploading Files

On the middle right corner is the list of created draft documents by default. You can click on the item to open it on the right pane.


You can edit the content anytime as long as it is on draft. The data are auto saved.


This grey box is a dropable area for files or you can click the button to select files. Uploading files are done seamlessly when you are online. Just Select your file, and thats its!


An indicator will tell you if your files are successfully uploaded to the cloud server.


You can open and update your file as long as it is not yet published. But make sure to update it by clicking the yellow button.

File History

On the right side of search bar, click the cloud icon to view all your file uploads.



Successful upload will have a download button for you to manually download a copy of your file.
